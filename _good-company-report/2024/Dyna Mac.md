<h2><strong>DYNA-MAC</strong></h2>
<p>Fuel your career with Dyna-Mac – where innovation and energy converge.</p>
<p>A global leader in the marine &amp; offshore energy industry, Dyna-Mac
performs Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) of offshore facilities
and onshore plants across the globe. At the forefront of the renewables
and green energy industry, it works with major international energy giants
in areas such as liquefied natural gas (LNG), green hydrogen, and ammonia.</p>
<p>Headquartered in Singapore, and listed on the Mainboard of the Singapore
Exchange, Dyna-Mac houses state-of-the-art facilities to cater to its diverse
global clientele.</p>
<p>Ready to transform your career into a realm of endless possibilities?
Watch this video to find out what it’s like to work at Dyna-Mac.</p>
<p>This video is filmed and produced by students from Ngee Ann Polytechnic,
Singapore Polytechnic and Temasek Polytechnic.</p>
